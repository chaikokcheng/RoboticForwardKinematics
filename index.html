Overview

This educational project demonstrates how robot arms determine the position of their end-effector (hand) based on joint angles. We've created three videos that progressively explain forward kinematics, starting from simple analogies for junior school students to mathematical derivations for undergraduate audiences.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand how robot arms move their end-effector to reach desired positions
  • Learn the relationship between joint angles and end-effector position
  • Explore mathematical foundations of forward kinematics
  • Apply transformation matrices to solve kinematic problems
